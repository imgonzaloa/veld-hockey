FAQ

Questions,
answered.

Is Veld really free right now?

Yes. Every feature is free during the Beta, with no credit card and no commitment. The Beta runs until July 2026, after which paid plans begin — but anything you set up now stays yours.

Which languages does Veld support?

Veld is fully trilingual — English, Spanish and Dutch — so players, coaches and staff can each use the app in their own language across the same club.

Is there an Android version?

Veld is available on iOS today, with Android coming soon. You can also use the full platform in any browser at veld-field-pro.base44.app.

How does the Marketplace work?

Players and coaches build a public profile; clubs post open positions. Everyone can search and filter by country, level, age and position — directly, with no agents or middlemen. Clubs include Marketplace access for all their players.

Can a Sporting Director oversee every team?

Yes. Club plans include a Sporting Director role with oversight across every squad — fixtures, availability, statistics and recruitment for the whole club from one dashboard.

Who owns the data we put into Veld?

Your club does. Your squads, stats and history belong to you, and you can keep using everything you build during the Beta when paid plans begin.
