Privacy Policy

How we protect your data on Veld — the global field hockey platform

📊 Data We Collect

To provide our services, we collect and store:

  • Name and photo
  • Position, level, and playing statistics
  • Performance data and match reports
  • Training session data and feedback
  • Team communications and messages

🔑 Who Can See Your Data

Access to data inside Veld is strictly role-based:

RoleCan access
PlayerOwn profile, stats, evaluations, messages
CoachOwn team(s), players, reports, evaluations
Sporting DirectorAll club teams, all coaches, club reports, club evaluations
Club AdminAll club data, teams, reports, organization settings

🛡️ Extra Protection for Minors

Players under 18 years old receive additional privacy protections:

  • Their profile is never visible in the Marketplace
  • Other users cannot search for or find them
  • Only their coach and club staff can see their data
  • Their identity is protected across all features

⚖️ Your Privacy Rights

Control your visibility. Change your marketplace visibility anytime in Settings → Privacy. You can hide your profile completely or limit who sees it.

Delete your account. You can request account deletion in the app under Settings → About. Your account and data will be permanently removed within 30 days.

Request your data. Email hello@veld-hockey.com to request a copy of your data or clarification on what we store.

🌍 GDPR & Data Protection

Veld complies with the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and international data protection standards.

  • Your data is stored securely with encryption
  • We never sell or share your data with third parties
  • We use data only to operate and improve Veld
  • You have rights to access, correct, and delete your data
  • Legitimate interest and explicit consent are our legal basis
